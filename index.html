Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a tutor?

Sign up, complete your profile with your expertise and experience, and start connecting with students. Our verification process ensures quality and trust.

Is SkillSwap free?

Creating an account is free. Tutors set their own rates, and we charge a small service fee to maintain the platform and ensure quality service.

How do I schedule a lesson?

Browse tutors, select one that matches your needs, and use our scheduling system to book a time slot. You can communicate with the tutor before the session.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel or reschedule a session up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Our flexible cancellation policy ensures fairness for both students and tutors.
