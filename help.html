Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with SkillSwap?

Getting started is easy! First, create an account and complete your profile. Then, you can either browse available tutors or post your own request for help. You can also offer your skills to help others.

How does the payment system work?

SkillSwap offers both monetary and skill-exchange payment options. For monetary transactions, we use secure payment processing. For skill exchanges, both parties agree on the terms before starting.

How can I ensure a safe exchange?

We recommend using our platform's messaging system, checking user reviews, and clearly defining expectations before starting. Never share personal financial information outside our secure platform.

What if I'm not satisfied with a session?

If you're not satisfied, first try to communicate with your exchange partner. If issues persist, our support team can help mediate. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee for paid sessions.
